Trippin AI is a Discord community assistant for beta testing, incident capture, workflow logs, and project feedback.

These pages are prepared for public static hosting so Discord app users can access clear policy information.

## Safety Summary

- Trippin AI does not provide investment advice.
- Trippin AI does not provide financial guarantees.
- Trippin AI does not perform live trading.
- Trippin AI does not handle wallet keys or private keys.
- Users should not submit secrets, passwords, tokens, private personal information, or sensitive data.
