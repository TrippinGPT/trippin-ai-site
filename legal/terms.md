# Trippin AI Terms of Service

Last updated: June 13, 2026

These Terms of Service are project policy drafts for Trippin AI. They are not legal advice.

## Product

Trippin AI is a Discord community assistant for beta testing, incident capture, workflow logs, and project feedback.

Trippin AI may reference OpenClaw as its internal engine or workflow system. OpenClaw is used to support project workflows, automation, and documentation; it is not marketed as a magic autonomous system.

## Acceptable Use

Use Trippin AI for community testing, build notes, incident reports, feedback, workflow logs, and educational project discussion.

Do not use Trippin AI to submit or request:

- Secrets
- Passwords
- Tokens
- Wallet keys
- Private keys
- Private personal information
- Sensitive personal data
- Payment card data
- Government identification
- Illegal content or instructions
- Harassment, abuse, or harmful content

## Safety Boundaries

Trippin AI does not provide investment advice, financial guarantees, live trading, wallet handling, private key handling, or professional advice.

Trippin AI is not a financial advisor, legal advisor, medical advisor, tax advisor, or professional service provider. Bot output may be incomplete, incorrect, or imperfect and should be reviewed by a human before relying on it.

Users are responsible for what they post in Discord. Do not post secrets, credentials, private documents, wallet/private keys, or sensitive information.

## Content and Community Moderation

Server admins may remove content, restrict commands, remove access, or take other moderation actions for safety, community, policy, or operational reasons.

Trippin AI may process user-submitted messages and command inputs to operate bot features. Users should only submit content they are comfortable sharing in the relevant Discord channel or beta workflow.

## Beta Status

Trippin AI is currently a beta project. Features may change, fail, produce fallback output, or be removed.

No uptime, feature availability, data retention, or outcome guarantees are provided.

## Prohibited Financial Use

Trippin AI does not execute trades, connect wallets, request wallet/private keys, perform swaps, provide buy/sell recommendations, or promise financial outcomes.

Any market, business, creative, or workflow discussion is for research, education, reporting, testing, or community purposes only.
